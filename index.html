Interesting facts about cats and why we love them.

Sleep schedule

Cats usually sleep around an average of 15 hours PER day. This means that a cat spends roughly 70% of their lives sleeping.

Abraham Lincoln, the 16th President of the United States, absolutely loved cats and would play with them for hours. Lincoln owned several cats during his time in the White House.

Whiskers

Cat whiskers are embedded deep in their body and are connected to the cat’s sensitive muscular and nervous systems, acting as touch receptors (or a “kitty radar”). Whiskers allow a cat to detect and respond to changes in their surroundings.

Additionally, not only do cats have whiskers on their face, but they also have a set of whiskers on the back of their front legs.

Teeth and toes

As kittens, they have 26 deciduous, or “baby”, teeth. As adult cats, they have 30 permanent teeth.

Most cats have 18 toes—5 on their front paws, and 4 on their back paws. However, some cats can be born with extra toes, a condition called polydactylism.
